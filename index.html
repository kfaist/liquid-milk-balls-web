About

The Mirror's Echo is a networked interactive work exploring presence, temporality, and reciprocity through real-time video transmission.

The work implements a temporal access model: seven minutes of unmediated experience, followed by progressive visual markers that persist indefinitely at peak intensity as watermarks.
